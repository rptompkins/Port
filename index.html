Loopr Music Pad

Loopr is a music pad HTML5 Web Audio app. Mapped to each key are specific sounds, loops, and samples, all synchronized and beat-matched under the selected tempo. The user selects which sounds to combine, forming their own beat and changing it or layering it as they choose. Inspired by the Novation Launchpad hardware controller.

HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript.

Dashboard

Dashboard is a login and registration app that allows users to create an account and store their information in a database. They can then log in and view their profile information, as well as go to other profile pages and leave messages and comments. Built on the LAMP stack.

HTML5, CSS, SQL, and PHP/CodeIgniter.
